Financial Education Revolution

Schools teach you to work for money, but don't teach you how to make money work for you. The wealthy focus on building assets that generate passive income.

Move from Employee (E) or Self-Employed (S) to Business Owner (B) or Investor (I) to achieve true financial freedom and leverage your time.
